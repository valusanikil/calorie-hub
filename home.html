Healthy Eating Tips

  • Focus on whole foods like fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Avoid processed foods and sugary snacks to maintain steady energy levels.
  • Eat balanced meals with a mix of protein, carbs, and healthy fats.
  • Practice mindful eating – enjoy your food and avoid distractions.
